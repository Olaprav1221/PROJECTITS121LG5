OUR STORY


Audi is one of the groups under the Volkwagen Group and is the group that was assigned control of Lamborghini, Bentley, Ducati and Italdesign by Volkswagen.

The Audi name signifies high-quality vehicles, forward-looking and sustainable drive technology, first-class quality, and progressive design worldwide.

Audi was founded in 1910 by August Horch, who initially founded his own business: August Horch & Cie, but left the company due to a difference of opinions with the board of directors.

The Modern Logo of the company originated from the Merger of 4 different German automotive brands, Audi, DKW, Horch, and Wanderer, to create the Auto Union AG which later became the Audi AG of today.
