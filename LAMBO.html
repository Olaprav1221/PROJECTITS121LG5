OUR STORY


Lamborghini is among the multiple brands under the Volkswagen Group.

Lamborghini, founded in 1963, is headquartered in SantAgata Bolognese, in Northeastern Italy. There it manufactures some of the worlds most sought-after super sports cars. With a constant ambition to deliver best-in-class automobiles, Lamborghini continues to set milestones in the history of luxury super sports cars.
