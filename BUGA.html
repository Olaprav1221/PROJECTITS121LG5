OUR STORY


Bugatti has a rich history steeped in innovation and performance. Founded in 1909 by Ettore Bugatti, the company quickly gained renown for its high-performance automobiles. Based initially in Molsheim, Alsace, Bugatti's cars were celebrated for their unparalleled design aesthetics and engineering prowess.

Over the years, Bugatti's legacy was defined not only by its remarkable vehicles but also by its dominance in motorsport, boasting numerous race victories. Today, Bugatti continues to uphold its tradition of excellence, crafting some of the most exclusive and sought-after supercars in the world.
